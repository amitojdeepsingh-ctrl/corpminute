⚠️ Is your corporation compliant?

Every incorporated Canadian business is legally required to maintain annual resolutions under the Canada Business Corporations Act (CBCA) and provincial equivalents. Failure to do so can jeopardize your corporate shield, complicate due diligence during a sale or financing, and result in CRA penalties. Most small business owners don't know — until it's expensive to fix.
